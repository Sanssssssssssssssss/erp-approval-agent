Purchase requisitions require:

- approval request details
- requester identity
- line items
- amount and amount threshold review
- cost center
- business purpose
- budget evidence
- vendor onboarding status
- supplier risk status
- quote or price basis
- contract/framework agreement or documented non-applicability
- procurement policy
- approval matrix
- split-order check

For standard office equipment purchases, a supplier quote may be used as price basis when it is tied to an active vendor and an applicable framework agreement or documented policy exception.
