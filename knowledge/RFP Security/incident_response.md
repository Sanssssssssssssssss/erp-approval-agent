# Incident response commitments

## Notification

Security incidents involving customer data are notified without undue delay and no later than 24 hours after confirmation.
Customer communications include incident scope, containment status, and next update timing.

## Recovery objectives

The documented recovery time objective is 4 hours.
The documented recovery point objective is 15 minutes.

## Exercises and review

The security team runs at least one tabletop exercise every year.
Material incidents receive a written root-cause analysis and corrective action plan.
